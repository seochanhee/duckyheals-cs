Key Features

Flower Focus Timer

Watch a flower bloom as you focus. Session logs, subject switching, and Pomodoro-style focus and break rhythms keep momentum going.

Duck Collection

Unlock 12 unique duck characters as your study streak grows. Each milestone hatches a new companion to cheer you on.

Spaced-Repetition Flashcards

The science-backed SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm schedules reviews at the moment you are about to forget, so knowledge really sticks.

Real-Time Study Groups

Join study rooms and see who is focusing right now. Preset cheer messages keep it safe and encouraging for every age group.

35 Achievements

Milestones for hours studied, streaks maintained, cheers sent, and more. Every session moves you forward.

Shareable Study Cards

Beautiful 1080 x 1920 cards optimized for Instagram Stories. Celebrate your progress with friends.
